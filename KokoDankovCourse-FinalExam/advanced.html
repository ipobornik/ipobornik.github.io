Welcome to another Arcade Highlight. Today we are featuring the turn based tactical strategy game, Elite Agents. In Elite Agent If you like the idea of blasting apart several marines in one shot, the rocket launcher is the way to go. If you prefer finesse in your combat, flash grenades will be your tool of choice, disabling and blinding your enemy. The goal of the game is to earn points by eliminating marines, capturing the flag, and holding the hill. You can only move one marine at a time and each marine can only take a limited number of actions per turn. Moving, attacking, switching weapons -- everything deducts from your total action value, so planning ahead is a must for you squad to be the most effective.
